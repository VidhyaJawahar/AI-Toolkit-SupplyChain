Quiz Questions

  1. What is a supply chain?
    Answer: A system that connects production, handling, and distribution of goods from raw material to end customer.
  2. Name one challenge in supply chain management.
    Answer: Demand forecasting.
  3. How can AI help in supply chains?
    Answer: By improving prediction, optimizing logistics, and enhancing decision-making.
  4. What is inventory management?
    Answer: The process of ordering, storing, and using a company's inventory efficiently.

Flashcards

Q: What is lead time?

A: Time between order placement and delivery.

Q: Define logistics.

A: Coordination of complex operations involving people, facilities, and supplies.

Q: AI application in SCM?

A: Predictive analytics, smart warehousing, demand forecasting.

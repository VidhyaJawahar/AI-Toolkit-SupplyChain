📘 Supply Chain Management - Lesson Plan

Objectives | Modules | References | Online Resources

🎯 Objectives

  • Understand the concept and importance of Supply Chain Management (SCM).
  • Learn about the key processes and elements of a supply chain.
  • Identify real-world applications of SCM in various industries.

📚 Lesson Modules

  1. Introduction to Supply Chain Management: Definition, importance, and examples.
  2. Components: Suppliers, manufacturers, warehouses, distribution centers, retailers.
  3. Functions: Planning, sourcing, making, delivering, and returning.
  4. SCM Strategies: Efficient vs. responsive supply chains, inventory management.
  5. Role of Technology: ERP, logistics tools, and automation.
  6. Case Study: Real-world example like Amazon or Walmart.
  7. Activity: Group discussion or mini presentation.
